# chantdiscography
is for managing the transfer of the chantdiscography.com database and user interface of chantdiscography.com. 
The creator of chantdiscography.com, Fr. Jerome Weber, made clear his desire to transfer this database to the Sacred Music program at the University of Notre Dame, 
and therefore I am managing this process. 
Peter Jeffery, Michael P. Grace Professor of Medieval Studies, Professor of Musicology and Ethnomusicology, past Director of the graduate program in Sacred Music, University of Notre Dame.
